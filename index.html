Do you believe in a livable, inclusive, safe, resilient and sustainable future housing?

Slums are cheracterized by; inadequate housing, lack of basic services, insecurities, tenure risk and exposure to environmental and security hazards.We are looking forward to intergrating slums into the wider urban fabric. This can be done by improving their connectivity and accessibility to the rest of the city, providing adequate infrastracture and services such as water, sanitation, energy, waste management, public transport, health , education and crucial needs a person should have. This will also improve air quality and green space wich is crucial and important to inhabitants of any environment.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire, empower, and engage individuals, communities, and urban planners in the pursuit of Sustainable Development Goal 11: Making cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable.
We are committed to fostering innovation, sharing knowledge, and advocating for sustainable urban practices that enhance the quality of life in cities worldwide.

Our Vision

Our vision at BejLivableFuture is a world where cities are vibrant, equitable, and harmonious places to live, work, and thrive.
We envision urban areas that prioritize environmental sustainability, social inclusivity, economic prosperity, and resilience, creating a more livable future for all.

Aspiration

Let's build cities that embrace diversity, foster innovation, and prioritize sustainability. Together, we can create urban spaces that are not just places to live but vibrant communities that thrive, leaving a lasting legacy of prosperity, inclusivity, and harmony for future generations.

Our Services


Affordable Housing Solutions

We promote affordable housing options for all, addressing issues of housing affordability and homelessness.
We connect with realtors and constructon engineers and build/own an affordable home as per your budget.This includes the creation of new affordable housing projects, affordable apartment complexes, or rehabilitation of existing housing stock.

Urban Planning and Design

We Provide expertise in urban planning and design, helping communities create well-organized, sustainable, and aesthetically pleasing spaces.
We do this in collaboration with local stakeholders such as municipal authorities, civil society organizations, urban planners, architects and the ministry of housing; county government.

Resilience Planning

Help communities prepare for and mitigate the impacts of climate change and natural disasters through resilience planning and disaster management strategies.
It involves strategies and actions aimed at enhancing a community's ability to withstand and recover from various adverse events, including natural disasters and other shocks.

Education and Workshops

It creates awareness and educate individuals and organizations about sustainable urban practices.
It is essential for equipping individuals and communities with the knowledge/skills needed to advance SDG 11 and contribute to the development of sustainable cities and communities.

Careers

Architect: Design and plan sustainable buildings and structures that contribute to eco-friendly urban development.

Sustainability Consultant: Advise businesses and municipalities on sustainable practices and help them align their operations well.

Project Manager:Oversee and coordinate the implementation of sustainable urban development projects.

Data Analyst for Urban Planning: Utilize data analysis to assess and monitor progress toward SDG 11 objectives.

Events/Programmes

Community Clean-up Days:Organize community clean-up events to improve local environments, promote cleanliness, and raise awareness about the impact of waste on urban areas.

Tree Planting Campaigns:Initiate tree planting campaigns to enhance green spaces, improve air quality, and contribute to overall environmental sustainability

Sustainable Home Design Competitions:Host competitions challenging residents to design and showcase sustainable home and garden solutions, promoting eco-friendly architecture and landscaping.

Community Workshops on Urban Gardening:Provide workshops on urban gardening techniques, such as container gardening and vertical gardening, to encourage residents to grow their own food.

Film Screenings and Discussions:Host screenings of documentaries or films related to urban sustainability, followed by discussions on how community members can contribute to positive change.

Educational Webinars: Host webinars featuring experts and thought leaders in the field of sustainable urban development, providing insights and information to a broader audience.

Youth Engagement Programs: Develop programs and events specifically targeted at engaging youth in sustainable practices and urban planning, fostering a sense of responsibility and involvement.

Community Surveys and Feedback Sessions:Gather feedback from residents through surveys and feedback sessions to understand their needs and concerns related to urban development.
